    For organisations

    Humanitarian goods are as vital as financial support. Please consider offering those as a form of donation. We graciously accept certain types of products, which your organization may wish to supply, and we regularly provide updates of the items most needed.

    Current priorities include:

    • Medical supplies: CAT or SOF tourniquets, IFAK first-aid kits, Celox powder;
    • Food: dried and canned food, chocolate, energy and dried fruit bars, baby food;
    • Electronics: pocket torches, battery packs, power banks;
    • Clothing: men’s socks, khaki-coloured T-shirts, belts;
